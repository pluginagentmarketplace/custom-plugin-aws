# Engineering Leadership Guide

## DORA Metrics

| Metric | Elite | High |
|--------|-------|------|
| Deployment Freq | Multiple/day | Weekly |
| Lead Time | <1 hour | <1 week |
| MTTR | <1 hour | <1 day |
| Change Fail Rate | <15% | <30% |

## 1-on-1 Framework

1. **Check-in**: How are you?
2. **Progress**: What's going well?
3. **Blockers**: What's challenging?
4. **Growth**: Career goals?
5. **Feedback**: For me/team?

## Team Topologies

- **Stream-aligned**: Product delivery
- **Platform**: Internal services
- **Enabling**: Capability building
- **Complicated-subsystem**: Specialists

## Leadership Styles

| Style | When |
|-------|------|
| Directing | New team members |
| Coaching | Growing skills |
| Supporting | Experienced team |
| Delegating | High performers |

# Management & Leadership

## Quick Start

Transition from individual contributor to engineering leader.

## Team Management Fundamentals

### Leadership Styles

**Directive** - Clear instructions, control
- Use when: Crisis, new team members
- Avoid when: Experienced team

**Coaching** - Guide and develop
- Use when: Developing talent
- Avoid when: Time-sensitive

**Supportive** - Empower and support
- Use when: Experienced team
- Avoid when: Clear guidance needed

**Delegative** - Hands-off
- Use when: High capability and motivation
- Avoid when: Low capability

### Hiring & Onboarding

**Hiring Process**
- Define role clearly
- Technical assessment
- Culture fit evaluation
- Reference checks

**Onboarding**
- Structured plan
- Mentorship assignment
- Clear expectations
- Gradual responsibility increase

### Feedback & Performance

**Effective Feedback**
- Specific, not vague
- Actionable

- Two-way conversation

**Performance Management**
- Regular 1-on-1s

- Annual reviews
- Development planning
